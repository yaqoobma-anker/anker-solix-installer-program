Program Requirements

What you need to join and what we need to verify installations.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Valid W9 form and business registration
  • Active Electrical Contractor License (or state equivalent)
  • Complete Anker SOLIX certification training
  • Signed Partner Agreement (pricing confidentiality, brand guidelines, quality commitment)
  • Solar, generator, electrical, or EV charging installation experience preferred

Installation Verification Checklist

  • Pre-installation photos (install location, electrical panel)
  • Post-installation photos (full product view, wiring close-up, nameplate/serial number)
  • System operation screenshot (App status page)
  • Permit copy (where locally required)
  • Installation must comply with product manual specifications
